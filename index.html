Labyrinth

King Minos of Crete has sentenced you to death by the Minotaur, a fearsome beast that dwells within the Labyrinth built by Daedalus. To survive, you must navigate the twisting corridors of the maze. If you reach the center and slay the Minotaur, you will claim King Minos' greatest treasure, long guarded at the heart of the Labyrinth-and it will be yours for the taking.

But beware, the Labyrinth is a treacherous place, filled with more monsters than just the Minotaur. You will need to use your wits and your skills to survive.
